ABOUT HUKARI KO BAI

Hukari ko bai Restaurant, is a local small business that first opened its doors in 2017. We were the first sushi restaurant in Valencia City. Our chefs take pride and pleasure in creating and presenting you the finest Japanese dishes, using the freshest and highest quality ingredients. Our restaurant features cozy dining areas and an outside patio as well as our sushi bar, hibachi tables and a private room for parties where you can enjoy a variety of chef specialties along with entertainment. Enjoy the finest in steak, chicken and seafood presented over an open flame with great flair by our expert chefs as well as the wide variety of our fresh sushi and rolls. Be sure to look at our menu; there is something to satisfy all tastes to include traditional, contemporary and special signature dishes and desserts. We have a private room for dining, business meetings and special occassions. The room comes with free wi-fi, a screen projector and a professional karaoke machine. And our newest addition, an outdoor patio with seating for 25, gives you the option to eat Shinsen ni (al fresco).






JAPANESE FUSION AT ITS FINEST

Bringing you a taste of culinary perfection in the art of Japanese cuisine, we have dared to combine traditional and modern techniques of Japanese cooking to give an exciting and unique taste to your favorite dishes. Marvel at our top chefs as they skillfully prepare your meal using nothing but the finest and freshest fruits of the sea. Go ahead and indulge in our extensive selection of sushi, sashimi, and seafood variety exclusively from Tokyo’s Tsukiji Fish Market. Top off your dining experience with our selection of sake specialties to complement every bite of your meal. Experience the best in Japanese cooking, dine with us today, here at Hukari ko bai restaurant.
